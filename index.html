About me

I am a Colombian Audio Engineer based in Berlin, I have been working in most areas of Audio for the last 20 years. I have been teaching Audio for the last 17 years in Private Institutions and in my Blog and Youtube channel "Directo Al Punto", I also manage my own studio in Colombia and berlin "Origen Audio", and last but not least, I am also a family man which is the best part of me and a lover of vintage video games.

Music Production
I have been involved in many roles as Producer, Composer, Arranger, Engineer and Musician on over 50 studio albums, 150 singles and 30 Soundtracks, Including Music for DW, Chevron Texaco, Griyo and many more; I'm also a certified Pro Tools Expert 310P.
Film Post
I have participated in many film and video productions as a composer, audio editor, video editor, re-recording engineer, even as a cameraman. I'm a Certified Pro Tools Expert in Film Post 310P and a Media Composer Instructor ACI100.
Video Games
I met this amazing world here in Berlin and I've been working in dialog editing for video games for the last 4 years, participating in big projects like Cyberpunk 2077, Fortnite, The Chant, and many others; I'm also a certified Pro Tools User in Game Audio.
Live
Live mixing is one of my passions, combined with my main job in studio projects, I mix often for many Bands, Venues and Organizations. as Kaffehaus Mila, Fete de la Musique, Mariachi Internacional El Dorado, Griyo, Calaca and many others.
